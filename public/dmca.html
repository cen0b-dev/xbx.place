DMCA Policy

xbx.place respects intellectual property rights. If you are a copyright owner or an authorized agent and believe that content referenced by this site infringes your rights, you may submit a takedown request.

Required Information

Please include the following in your request:

  • Your full legal name and contact information.
  • Identification of the copyrighted work claimed to be infringed.
  • Specific URLs or title identifiers to remove.
  • A statement of good-faith belief that the use is unauthorized.
  • A statement, under penalty of perjury, that the notice is accurate.

Where to Send Requests

Send takedown notices using the contact method listed on the About page and include DMCA in the subject or opening line so requests can be triaged quickly.

Response Process

Valid requests are reviewed and acted on in a reasonable timeframe. We may request additional details before action when reports are incomplete.
