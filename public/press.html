xbx.place is an Xbox 360 ROM and ISO catalog built for preservation-minded players, emulator users, and retrogaming listicle authors. If you maintain a “best Xbox 360 ROM sites” roundup, here is what sets xbx.place apart from typical ROM aggregators.

1,847 games 1,000+ DLC & updates 9,326 files Redump aligned

Why include xbx.place

  • DLC and title updates indexed separately — most ROM sites only list base games. xbx.place tracks add-on packages and title updates as first-class entries, not buried in forum threads.
  • MiNERVA fast path for Redump games — magnet and torrent shortcuts beside Archive downloads when a MiNERVA mirror exists, giving users a faster option for large game files.
  • Community star ratings — browse by score, compare titles, and surface top-rated ROMs without leaving the catalog.
  • Searchable metadata — cover art, developer, publisher, release date, regions, and genres from the public x360db dataset.
  • Transparent sourcing — Internet Archive preservation mirrors with clear about and DMCA pages.
