About xbx.place

xbx.place is an index-style catalog for Xbox 360 titles and related downloadable content. It is designed to make discovery easier with fast search, title metadata, ratings, and artwork.

Data and Sources

Title metadata and artwork references are aggregated from public Xbox 360 datasets and catalog tooling used by this project. Download mappings are generated from archive indexes and merged into the site catalog during the data build pipeline.

How Downloads Are Handled

Download links in the UI are routed through a proxy endpoint to avoid exposing raw upstream URLs directly in the client interface.
